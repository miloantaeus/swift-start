# Upwork Proposal Prompts

## AI PROMPTS FOR UPWORK PROPOSALS (30+)

### Research Prompts
- "Research [industry] and find the top 5 challenges facing [job title] in 2026..."
- "Find all federal grant opportunities under $50K for [focus area] on grants.gov..."
- "Conduct a competitive analysis of [3 competitors] — pricing, positioning, weaknesses..."

### Writing Prompts
- "Write a cold outreach email to [person] about [specific problem they've posted]..."
- "Draft a grant proposal narrative for [organization] applying to [funder] for [amount]..."
- "Create an executive summary from these research notes [paste notes]..."

### Analysis Prompts
- "Analyze this dataset [paste data] and identify the top 3 actionable insights..."
- "Review this client's current workflow [describe] and identify 5 automation opportunities..."
- "Score this lead [describe] using the client qualification rubric..."

### Communication Prompts
- "Write a follow-up email to [person] who saw my proposal on [date]..."
- "Draft a scope change request for a project where [description]..."
- "Write a client onboarding message for a new [service type] engagement..."
