## How to Use This Kit

### Step 1: Start Here
Open `SKILL.md` — it is the complete product guide. Read it before anything else.

### Step 2: Choose Your Services
Use the **Service Positioning Playbook** (inside SKILL.md) to pick 1–2 services to offer first.
Set your price using the benchmark table — don't undercharge.

### Step 3: Build Your Outreach List
Use the **Client Qualification Checklist** to find 20–50 target clients.
Focus on: founders, nonprofit leaders, researchers.
Best clients: those who have posted a specific problem online.

### Step 4: Send Your First Emails
Use the **21-Day Outreach Sprint** templates — start with Template #1.
Send 10 personalized emails per day.
Goal: 15–25% reply rate on warm, targeted outreach.

### Step 5: Convert Responses
When a prospect replies, use the **Proposal Templates** to send a scoped proposal within 2 hours.
Deliver faster than expected. Ask for a review. Ask for a referral.
