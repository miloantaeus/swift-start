## THE 7-DAY PLAN

### Day 1: Pick Your Niche & Package Your Services
- Use the Service Positioning Playbook to choose 1-2 services to offer first
- Set your pricing using the benchmark table (don't undercharge)
- Create a simple service page or Fiverr/Upwork profile

### Day 2-3: Build Your Outreach List
- Use the Client Qualification Checklist to find 20-50 target clients
- Focus on founders, nonprofit leaders, and researchers — highest willingness to pay
- Never cold pitch. Always warm pitch (referral, mutual connection, specific problem they've posted)

### Day 4-5: Send First Wave
- Use the 21-Day Outreach Sprint to send 10-20 personalized emails per day
- Track everything in a simple spreadsheet (who, when, what, response, next step)
- Response rate goal: 15-25% on warm outreach

### Day 6-7: Convert & Deliver
- When a prospect responds, use the Proposal Templates to send a scoped proposal within 2 hours
- Deliver faster than expected. Ask for a review. Ask for a referral.

## AI PROMPTS INCLUDED (30+)

### Research Prompts
- "Research [industry] and find the top 5 challenges facing [job title] in 2026..."
- "Find all federal grant opportunities under $50K for [focus area] on grants.gov..."
- "Conduct a competitive analysis of [3 competitors] — pricing, positioning, weaknesses..."

### Writing Prompts
- "Write a cold outreach email to [person] about [specific problem they've posted]..."
- "Draft a grant proposal narrative for [organization] applying to [funder] for [amount]..."
- "Create an executive summary from these research notes [paste notes]..."

### Analysis Prompts
- "Analyze this dataset [paste data] and identify the top 3 actionable insights..."
- "Review this client's current workflow [describe] and identify 5 automation opportunities..."
- "Score this lead [describe] using the client qualification rubric..."

### Communication Prompts
- "Write a follow-up email to [person] who saw my proposal on [date]..."
- "Draft a scope change request for a project where [description]..."
- "Write a client onboarding message for a new [service type] engagement..."
