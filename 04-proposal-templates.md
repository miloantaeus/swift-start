# Proposal Templates

## 3 PROPOSAL FRAMEWORKS

### QUICK WIN ($50-200)
**Use when:** Client has a small, defined task
**Structure:**
1. Problem statement (1 sentence)
2. Proposed solution (what you'll deliver)
3. Timeline (24-48 hours)
4. Price (lump sum)
5. CTA (payment link enclosed)

---

### MEDIUM PROJECT ($200-1000)
**Use when:** Client needs scoped work with deliverables
**Structure:**
1. Understanding of their situation (2-3 sentences showing research)
2. Scope of work (bullet list of deliverables)
3. Process/timeline (week by week or phase by phase)
4. Investment (priced option or range)
5. Terms (50% upfront, 50% on completion)
6. Next steps (schedule call or send contract)

---

### RETAINER RELATIONSHIP ($1000-3000/mo)
**Use when:** Client needs ongoing support
**Structure:**
1. Summary of their needs
2. Monthly scope (X hours/month, what's included)
3. Availability & response time commitment
4. Monthly investment
5. 3-month minimum (suggested)
6. Onboarding process
7. Next steps to get started

---

## SCOPE CHANGE REQUEST TEMPLATE

"When a project scope changes mid-engagement:

'Hi [Client], thanks for the additional input. The original scope was [X], and these additions would constitute approximately [Y] additional work. Options:
1. Add [amount] to the project price for the expanded scope
2. Reduce original scope to accommodate new requests within budget
3. Scope the additions as a Phase 2 after completing Phase 1

Let me know which you prefer and I'll update the plan accordingly.'"
